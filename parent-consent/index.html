Parent Consent and Data Rights Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Gulsabi World, available at gulsabi.com, is operated by Pixnut Media Private Limited.

This Parent Consent and Data Rights Policy explains how parents and legal guardians can give consent, withdraw consent, review child-related personal information, correct it, and request deletion.

In this policy, “Gulsabi,” “Gulsabi World,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Pixnut Media Private Limited and the Gulsabi World website.

1. Purpose of this policy

Gulsabi is designed for children and families.

We want parents and legal guardians to stay in control of any personal information connected to their child. We will always try to help with genuine parent requests, and we handle them in line with this policy and applicable law.

This policy explains:

  • When parent consent may be required.
  • How parents can contact us.
  • How parents can ask to review data.
  • How parents can ask to correct data.
  • How parents can ask to delete data.
  • How parents can withdraw consent.
  • How we handle parent requests.

2. Who can give consent

Consent for a child must be given by the child’s parent or legal guardian.

A child should not create an account, join a waitlist, submit a form, or use child-specific features without permission from a parent or legal guardian.

If we believe a child has submitted personal information without proper consent, we may delete that information.

3. When parent consent may be needed

Parent consent may be needed before we collect, use, or store personal information about a child.

This may apply to features such as:

  • Child profiles.
  • Saved game progress.
  • Quiz or learning history.
  • Parent dashboard settings.
  • AI Buddy features, if launched.
  • Voice-based learning features, if launched.
  • Personalised learning paths, if launched.
  • Any feature that stores child-related personal information.

Most basic Gulsabi games can be played without creating an account.

4. Information that may need parent consent

If child-specific features are launched, we may request consent to process limited child-related information, such as:

  • Child’s first name or nickname.
  • Age or age range.
  • Game progress.
  • Quiz scores.
  • Learning activity history.
  • Time spent on activities.
  • Parent-selected topics.
  • Parent-selected safety settings.
  • Parent-selected screen time controls.

We will not ask children to provide unnecessary personal information.

5. Information children should not submit

Children should not submit private personal information on Gulsabi.

This includes:

  • Full name.
  • Home address.
  • Phone number.
  • School name.
  • Email address.
  • Passwords.
  • Photos.
  • Voice recordings.
  • Parent payment details.
  • Medical details.
  • Private family information.

Parents should contact us if they believe a child has shared private information by mistake.

Email: hello@pixnut.com

6. How parent consent may be collected

Depending on the feature, we may collect parent consent through one or more of the following methods:

  • Parent email confirmation.
  • Parent account approval.
  • Consent form.
  • Parent dashboard setting.
  • Confirmation before child-specific features are enabled.
  • Any other legally accepted consent method.

We may also take steps to check that the person giving consent is an adult parent or legal guardian.

7. Parent rights

Parents and legal guardians may contact us to:

  • Ask whether we hold personal information about their child.
  • Review child-related personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate child-related personal information.
  • Request deletion of child-related personal information.
  • Withdraw consent.
  • Stop further collection or use of child-related personal information.
  • Ask how child-related information is used.
  • Ask how long child-related information is kept.

To make a request, email:

hello@pixnut.com

8. How to submit a parent request

Parents can send a request by email.

Please include:

  • Parent or guardian name.
  • Parent email address.
  • Child’s first name or nickname, if relevant.
  • The request type, such as review, correction, deletion, or withdrawal.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • Any details needed to identify the account or feature.

Do not send sensitive child documents unless we specifically ask for them.

9. Verification of parent requests

Before acting on a request, we may verify that the person making the request is the child’s parent or legal guardian.

We may ask for reasonable information to confirm this.

We will not ask for more information than needed.

If we cannot verify the request, we may be unable to share, correct, or delete the requested information.

10. Review requests

A parent or legal guardian may ask to review personal information we hold about their child.

Where legally required and reasonably possible, we will provide access to the relevant child-related personal information.

We may refuse or limit access where disclosure could:

  • Harm another person’s privacy.
  • Reveal security information.
  • Break the law.
  • Affect the safety of a child or another user.
  • Disclose information we are legally allowed or required to withhold.

11. Correction requests

A parent or legal guardian may ask us to correct inaccurate child-related personal information.

After we verify the request, we will take reasonable steps to correct the information.

12. Deletion requests

A parent or legal guardian may ask us to delete child-related personal information.

After we verify the request, we will delete the relevant information unless we need to keep it for a legal, safety, security, or record-keeping reason.

If child-related information is deleted, some features may stop working.

For example:

  • Saved progress may be lost.
  • Parent dashboard settings may be removed.
  • AI Buddy history may no longer be available.
  • Personalised settings may reset.

13. Withdrawal of consent

A parent or legal guardian may withdraw consent at any time.

To withdraw consent, email: hello@pixnut.com

After consent is withdrawn, we will stop processing the relevant child-related personal information unless we are legally allowed or required to continue.

Some child-specific features may no longer work after consent is withdrawn.

14. Response timeline

We aim to respond to parent requests within 15 working days. This is a target we work towards, not a guarantee.

Some requests may take longer if:

  • The request is complex.
  • Verification is needed.
  • More information is required.
  • The request involves third-party service providers.
  • The request relates to legal or safety concerns.

If more time is needed, we will try to inform the parent or legal guardian.

15. Data that may be retained

Even after a deletion or consent withdrawal request, we may retain limited information where necessary.

This may include information needed for:

  • Legal compliance.
  • Safety records.
  • Security logs.
  • Fraud or misuse prevention.
  • Dispute handling.
  • Consent history.
  • Technical backup systems for a limited period.

We will not keep child-related personal information longer than needed.

16. No targeted advertising to children

We do not use child-related personal information for targeted advertising.

We do not sell children’s personal information.

We do not allow public child profiles or stranger messaging.

17. Parent dashboard and future features

If Gulsabi launches a parent dashboard, parents may be able to manage:

  • Child profile settings.
  • Topic preferences.
  • Screen time settings.
  • Activity history.
  • Blocked content.
  • AI Buddy settings, if launched.
  • Data deletion requests.
  • Consent settings.

Until those features are live, parents can contact us by email.

Email: hello@pixnut.com

18. AI Buddy and voice features

If Gulsabi launches AI Buddy or voice-based features, parental consent may be required before a child uses them.

Parents may be able to control:

  • Whether AI Buddy is enabled.
  • Which topics are allowed.
  • Which topics are blocked.
  • Whether activity summaries are saved.
  • How long activity data is retained.
  • Whether child-related AI data is deleted.

Children should not share private personal information with AI Buddy.

AI responses may sometimes be incorrect or unsuitable. Parents should supervise use of AI features.

20. Changes to this policy

We may update this Parent Consent and Data Rights Policy from time to time.

When we update it, we will change the “Last updated” date above.

If we make major changes to how parent consent or child-related personal information is handled, we will take reasonable steps to notify parents where required.
