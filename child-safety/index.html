Child Safety Commitment

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Gulsabi World, available at gulsabi.com, is operated by Pixnut Media Private Limited.

This Child Safety Commitment explains the safety measures we aim to follow for Gulsabi World.

Keeping children safe is at the heart of everything we build, and we are glad you are reading this. It describes the steps we aim to take.

This is a statement of our approach and our best efforts. It is not a promise, warranty, or guarantee that every online risk can be prevented or that any particular outcome will be achieved. No website can remove every risk. Online safety always depends on active parental supervision, and parents and legal guardians remain responsible for supervising their child’s use of Gulsabi and deciding what is suitable for their child.

In this document, “Gulsabi,” “Gulsabi World,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Pixnut Media Private Limited and the Gulsabi World website.

Nothing in this commitment creates legal obligations or warranties beyond those set out in our Terms and Conditions and applicable law.

1. Our safety approach

Gulsabi is created for children and families.

We design Gulsabi to support a safe, educational, and parent-guided experience.

Gulsabi may include:

  • Educational videos.
  • Browser games.
  • Stories.
  • Rhymes.
  • Quizzes.
  • Simple explanations.
  • Curiosity-led learning.
  • Future parent-controlled AI Buddy features, if launched.

Children should use Gulsabi with permission and supervision from a parent or legal guardian.

2. Age group

Gulsabi is mainly designed for children aged 3 to 7.

Some content may also be useful for older children, parents, and teachers.

Parents should decide whether the content is suitable for their child.

3. Parent supervision

Gulsabi is a learning and entertainment website. It is not a replacement for parents, guardians, teachers, or caregivers.

Parents and legal guardians are responsible for:

  • Allowing their child to use Gulsabi.
  • Supervising their child’s online activity.
  • Setting screen time limits.
  • Reviewing content suitability.
  • Helping children understand online safety.
  • Making sure children do not share private information.

4. Features Gulsabi is designed not to support

Gulsabi is designed not to support:

  • Open chat between children.
  • Stranger messaging.
  • Public child profiles.
  • Public comment posting by children.
  • User-generated child social feeds.
  • Direct contact between unknown adults and children.
  • Targeted advertising to children.
  • Collection of unnecessary child personal information.
  • Unsafe, adult, violent, or harmful content aimed at children.

If any community, account, or interactive feature is added in the future, we aim to review it for child safety and parent control before launch.

5. Information children should not share

Children should not share private information on Gulsabi.

Children should not submit:

  • Full name.
  • Home address.
  • Phone number.
  • Email address.
  • School name.
  • Photos.
  • Voice recordings.
  • Passwords.
  • Parent payment details.
  • Medical details.
  • Private family information.

Parents should contact us if they believe a child has shared private information by mistake.

Email: hello@pixnut.com

6. Content safety standards

Gulsabi content is intended to be suitable for young children.

We aim to avoid:

  • Adult themes.
  • Sexual content.
  • Graphic violence.
  • Hate speech.
  • Bullying.
  • Dangerous challenges.
  • Unsafe experiments without adult supervision.
  • Fear-based content.
  • Strong language.
  • Political persuasion aimed at children.
  • Religious persuasion aimed at children.
  • Misleading or harmful health claims.

Some educational topics may still need parent guidance. Parents should supervise children during learning activities.

7. Games safety

Gulsabi games are designed to be simple and child-friendly.

We aim to avoid:

  • Gambling-style mechanics.
  • Real-money rewards.
  • Loot boxes.
  • In-game purchases aimed at children.
  • Chat with strangers.
  • Unsafe competition pressure.
  • Violent gameplay.
  • Addictive pressure loops.

Basic games may save progress in the browser. Some progress may be lost if browser data is cleared.

8. Video safety

Gulsabi may include or link to educational videos.

Some videos may be hosted on third-party platforms, such as YouTube.

Parents should know:

  • Third-party platforms may show their own interface, recommendations, or settings.
  • We do not control third-party platforms.
  • Parents should supervise children when they watch videos.
  • Parents should review YouTube or device-level child safety settings.

9. Advertising and commercial safety

Gulsabi does not use children’s personal information for targeted advertising.

We do not sell children’s personal information.

If Gulsabi later offers paid features, subscriptions, or products, these will be intended for parents or guardians, not direct purchase by children.

Children should not enter payment details.

10. AI Buddy safety, if launched

Gulsabi may later offer an AI Buddy or voice-based companion.

If launched, AI Buddy will be designed for child-friendly learning and parent-supervised use.

AI Buddy may support:

  • Simple explanations.
  • Stories.
  • Rhymes.
  • Quizzes.
  • Curiosity-led questions.
  • Learning activities.

AI Buddy should not be used for:

  • Medical advice.
  • Legal advice.
  • Emergency help.
  • Mental health diagnosis.
  • Financial advice.
  • Adult topics.
  • Unsafe instructions.
  • Private personal disclosures.
  • Contacting strangers.

Parents should supervise AI Buddy use.

AI responses may sometimes be incorrect, incomplete, or unsuitable. Parents should guide children and check important answers.

11. Parent controls, if launched

If parent dashboard features are launched, parents may be able to control:

  • Child profile settings.
  • Screen time.
  • Allowed topics.
  • Blocked topics.
  • AI Buddy access.
  • Learning history.
  • Activity summaries.
  • Data deletion requests.
  • Consent settings.

Until these features are available, parents can contact us directly.

Email: hello@pixnut.com

12. Reporting unsafe content or concerns

Parents, guardians, teachers, or users may report:

  • Unsafe content.
  • Incorrect educational content.
  • Privacy concerns.
  • Child safety concerns.
  • Technical issues.
  • Misuse of Gulsabi content.
  • Suspected unauthorised child data collection.

Reports can be sent to:

hello@pixnut.com

Please include:

  • Page URL, if available.
  • Screenshot, if useful.
  • Short description of the concern.
  • Parent or guardian contact email.
  • Child’s personal details only if needed.

Do not send sensitive child documents unless we request them.

13. How we review reports

When we receive a child safety report, we may:

  • Review the reported page, game, video, or feature.
  • Remove or change content where needed.
  • Fix technical issues.
  • Restrict access to unsafe features.
  • Contact the reporting parent or guardian.
  • Take reasonable steps to reduce similar issues.
  • Escalate serious safety matters where required by law.

We aim to respond to child safety reports within 15 working days. This is a target we work towards, not a guarantee, and how we respond is at our reasonable discretion.

Some reports may take longer if they require technical review, third-party review, legal review, or more information from the reporting person.

14. Emergency situations

Gulsabi is not an emergency service.

If a child is in danger or needs urgent help, contact local emergency services immediately.

Do not rely on Gulsabi, email, AI Buddy, games, videos, or website forms for emergency support.

15. School and classroom use

Teachers and schools may use Gulsabi content for learning support, subject to our Terms and Conditions and Copyright Policy.

Teachers should ensure:

  • Children are supervised.
  • School internet safety rules are followed.
  • Children do not submit private information.
  • Third-party video platforms are used with care.
  • Printable or downloadable content is used only as allowed.

16. Accessibility and inclusion

We aim to make Gulsabi friendly and usable for children with different learning needs.

Where possible, we may support:

  • Clear visuals.
  • Simple language.
  • Readable text.
  • Short activities.
  • Parent-guided learning.
  • Captions or text support for videos, where available.

Parents can contact us with accessibility concerns.

Email: hello@pixnut.com

17. Child data and privacy

Child safety also includes privacy safety.

Our child data practices are explained in:

  • Privacy Policy.
  • Children’s Privacy Policy.
  • Parent Consent and Data Rights Policy.
  • Cookie Policy.

Parents should read these pages to understand how child-related information is handled.

18. Changes to this commitment

We may update this Child Safety Commitment from time to time.

When we update it, we will change the “Last updated” date above.

If we make major changes to child safety practices, we will take reasonable steps to update parents where required.
