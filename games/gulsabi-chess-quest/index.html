Learn Chess with Gulsabi Super

Gulsabi Chess Quest is a kids chess learning game by Gulsabi. It teaches chess pieces through lessons, practice missions, bot play, and 2-player chess.

What is Gulsabi Chess Quest?

Gulsabi Chess Quest is a chess learning adventure where kids learn pieces, solve missions, play with a bot, and enjoy 2-player chess — guided by Gulsabi Super.

Who is it for?

It is made for kids aged 5 and up who are starting to learn chess, and for families who want a safe, simple way to play together.

What does it teach?

  • How each chess piece moves
  • How to plan simple moves
  • How to protect the king
  • How to take turns
  • How to think before moving

What game modes are included?

Chess Lessons for Kids

A guided Learning Adventure with six lessons — Pawn Path, Rook Road, Bishop Bridge, Knight Jump, Queen Power, and King Safety.

Practice Chess Pieces

Pick any piece and complete practice missions to master how it moves.

Play Chess Against the Bot

Play a full game against a friendly browser-based bot with Easy, Smart, and Challenge levels.

Play 2 Player Chess

Two players share one device for a full game of chess with turns, captures, and check detection.

Does it need login?

Gulsabi Chess Quest works without login and stores simple progress on the device. It does not require personal details or chat features.
