About Gulsabi Chess Quest

Gulsabi Chess Quest is a free chess learning game for kids aged 5 and up, made by Gulsabi (Pixnut). It teaches how every chess piece moves through guided lessons and practice missions, then lets kids play a friendly browser-based bot or 2-player chess on one device. It needs no login, has no ads, and collects no personal details.

What kids learn

How each chess piece moves, how to plan simple moves, how to protect the king, how to take turns, and how to think before moving.

Game modes

Learning Adventure (six guided lessons), Practice Missions (test how each piece moves), Play vs Bot (easy, smart and challenge levels), and 2 Player Chess (same-device).

Frequently asked questions

What is Gulsabi Chess Quest? A chess learning game for kids with lessons, practice missions, bot play, and 2-player chess.

Can kids learn chess from this game? Yes. Kids learn how each chess piece moves through guided lessons and practice missions.

Does the game have a bot? Yes. Kids can play against a simple browser-based chess bot.

Can two players play on one device? Yes. The game includes a 2-player mode for same-device play.

Does the game need login? No. It works without login and saves simple progress on the device.

Is this game safe for kids? Yes. It does not require personal details or chat features.

What chess pieces does it teach? Pawn, rook, bishop, knight, queen, and king.
