Children's Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Gulsabi World, available at gulsabi.com, is operated by Pixnut Media Private Limited.

This Children’s Privacy Policy explains how we protect children’s privacy when they use Gulsabi World.

In this policy, “Gulsabi,” “Gulsabi World,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Pixnut Media Private Limited and the Gulsabi World website.

Gulsabi is designed for children and families. Our goal is to provide a safe, educational, and parent-friendly experience, with parents firmly in control of any information connected to their child.

We aim to collect as little child-related information as possible. At the same time, online safety depends on active parental supervision, and parents remain responsible for their child’s use of Gulsabi. This policy explains how we try to protect children and how parents can stay in control.

1. Who this policy is for

This policy is written for parents and legal guardians.

It applies when children use:

  • gulsabi.com
  • Gulsabi games
  • Gulsabi videos
  • Gulsabi learning content
  • Parent dashboard previews
  • Newsletter or waitlist features
  • Future Gulsabi AI Buddy features, if launched
  • Any other Gulsabi service that links to this policy

Children should use Gulsabi only with permission and supervision from a parent or legal guardian.

2. Our child privacy promise

We take children’s privacy seriously.

We do not:

  • Sell children’s personal information.
  • Show targeted advertising to children.
  • Allow strangers to message children.
  • Allow public child profiles.
  • Ask children to share their full name, address, phone number, school name, or precise location.
  • Ask children to upload private photos or documents.
  • Use children’s data for advertising profiles.
  • Knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent where consent is required.

Most basic Gulsabi games can be played without creating an account.

3. Information children can use Gulsabi without providing

Children can use many parts of Gulsabi without giving us personal information.

For example, children may be able to:

  • Play browser games.
  • Watch learning content.
  • Read pages.
  • Explore simple activities.
  • Use basic website features.

Some information may still be collected automatically for website security, performance, and basic analytics. This is explained below.

4. Information we may collect from parents

When a parent or legal guardian contacts us, signs up, or joins a waitlist, we may collect:

  • Parent name, if provided.
  • Parent email address.
  • Messages sent to us.
  • Newsletter or waitlist preferences.
  • Consent records, where parental consent is required.
  • Parent dashboard settings, if such features are launched.

We use this information to communicate with parents, manage consent, respond to requests, and provide parent-controlled services.

5. Information we may collect about children

We try to collect as little child-related information as possible.

If future features require child information, such as child profiles, saved progress, parent controls, or AI Buddy, we may collect limited information with parental consent where required.

This may include:

  • Child’s first name or nickname.
  • Age or age range.
  • Game progress.
  • Quiz scores.
  • Learning activity history.
  • Time spent on activities.
  • Parent-selected topics.
  • Parent-selected safety settings.
  • Parent-selected screen time controls.

We will not ask children to provide sensitive personal details.

6. Game and usage information

When someone uses Gulsabi, we may collect basic technical and usage information, such as:

  • Pages visited.
  • Games played.
  • Approximate session duration.
  • Device type.
  • Browser type.
  • General location, such as country or city.
  • Error logs.
  • Website performance data.

We use this information to keep the website working, improve content, fix errors, and protect the service.

7. Cookies and local storage

Gulsabi may use cookies or local storage.

These may be used to:

  • Save basic game progress.
  • Remember simple site settings.
  • Understand website usage.
  • Improve website performance.
  • Protect the website from misuse.

Some information may stay only on the user’s browser or device.

Parents can control cookies through browser settings. Some features may not work correctly if cookies or local storage are disabled.

8. YouTube videos and third-party content

Gulsabi may link to or embed videos from YouTube or other platforms.

When a child plays or opens content from a third-party platform, that platform may collect information according to its own privacy policy.

We do not control the privacy practices of YouTube or other third-party platforms.

Parents should supervise children when they watch videos or leave gulsabi.com.

9. Future AI Buddy features

Gulsabi may later offer an AI Buddy or voice-based companion.

If launched, this feature will be designed for child-friendly learning, stories, rhymes, quizzes, and simple explanations.

Before allowing children to use child-specific AI features, we may ask for parental consent where required.

Parents may be able to control:

  • Allowed topics.
  • Blocked topics.
  • Screen time.
  • Conversation settings.
  • Safety filters.
  • Activity history.
  • Data deletion requests.

Children should not share private personal information with the AI Buddy.

This includes:

  • Full name.
  • Address.
  • Phone number.
  • School name.
  • Passwords.
  • Family details.
  • Photos.
  • Payment information.
  • Medical information.

AI responses may sometimes be incorrect or unsuitable. Parents should supervise use of AI features.

10. How we use children’s information

We may use limited child-related information to:

  • Provide games and learning activities.
  • Save progress where enabled.
  • Show age-appropriate content.
  • Maintain parent controls.
  • Improve child safety.
  • Respond to parent requests.
  • Fix technical issues.
  • Prevent misuse.
  • Comply with legal duties.

We do not use children’s personal information for targeted advertising.

11. Parental consent

Where required by law, we will seek verifiable consent from a parent or legal guardian before collecting, using, or sharing a child’s personal information.

Parents may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

Email: hello@pixnut.com

If consent is withdrawn, some child-specific features may stop working.

12. Parent rights

Parents and legal guardians may contact us to:

  • Ask whether we hold personal information about their child.
  • Review child-related personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of child-related personal information.
  • Withdraw consent.
  • Stop further collection or use of the child’s personal information.
  • Ask questions about this policy.

To make a request, email us at:

hello@pixnut.com

We may ask for information to verify that the person making the request is the child’s parent or legal guardian.

13. Sharing children’s information

We do not sell children’s personal information.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us run Gulsabi.

These may include:

  • Website hosting providers.
  • Email service providers.
  • Analytics providers.
  • Security and error-monitoring providers.
  • AI or voice technology providers, if AI Buddy launches.
  • Customer support tools.
  • Legal or professional advisers.

These service providers may use the information only to provide services to us. They are not allowed to sell it or use it for their own advertising.

We may also share information if required by law, court order, government request, or to protect the safety and rights of children, parents, users, Gulsabi, or others.

14. Data retention

We keep children’s personal information only as long as needed.

For example:

  • Game progress may be kept while the feature is active.
  • Parent consent records may be kept as required by law.
  • Technical logs may be kept for a limited period for security and maintenance.
  • Parent messages may be kept as long as needed to respond and maintain records.

When information is no longer needed, we delete it or make reasonable efforts to remove identifying details.

Parents may request deletion by emailing: hello@pixnut.com

15. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information.

These may include:

  • Access controls.
  • Secure hosting.
  • Limited staff access.
  • Encrypted connections where available.
  • Security monitoring.
  • Regular review of privacy practices.

We take security seriously, but no website can guarantee complete security, and we cannot promise that information will always be safe. To the extent permitted by law, parents should help children avoid sharing private information online and supervise their use of Gulsabi.

16. Children should not contact us alone

If you are a child, please do not send us private information.

Do not send:

  • Your full name.
  • Your home address.
  • Your phone number.
  • Your school name.
  • Your photo.
  • Your voice recording.
  • Your password.
  • Your parent’s payment details.

Please ask your parent or guardian before contacting us.

17. International users

Gulsabi is operated from India.

Users from other countries may access the website. Information may be processed in India or in other countries where our service providers operate.

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information according to applicable law.

18. Changes to this policy

We may update this Children’s Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we update it, we will change the “Last updated” date above.

If we make major changes to how we handle children’s personal information, we will take reasonable steps to notify parents and seek consent where required.
