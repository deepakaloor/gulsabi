Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Gulsabi World, available at gulsabi.com, is operated by Pixnut Media Private Limited.

This Cookie Policy explains how Gulsabi World uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies.

In this policy, “Gulsabi,” “Gulsabi World,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Pixnut Media Private Limited and the Gulsabi World website.

We keep cookies on Gulsabi limited and purposeful — mainly to make the site work, keep it safe, and understand basic usage. Parents stay in control and can manage cookies through their browser at any time. Where we use cookies to operate, secure, and improve Gulsabi, we do so to the extent permitted by law.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on a device when someone visits a website.

Cookies help websites:

  • Work properly.
  • Remember basic settings.
  • Understand website usage.
  • Improve performance.
  • Keep the website safe.

Gulsabi may also use local storage. Local storage works in a similar way, but the information is saved inside the browser.

2. Why Gulsabi uses cookies and local storage

Gulsabi may use cookies and local storage to:

  • Run website features.
  • Save basic game progress.
  • Remember simple settings.
  • Understand which pages and games are used.
  • Fix website errors.
  • Improve loading speed.
  • Protect the website from misuse.
  • Support newsletter or waitlist forms.
  • Help embedded content work properly.

We do not use cookies to show targeted advertising to children.

3. Types of cookies we may use

A. Essential cookies

These cookies are needed for the website to work.

They may help with:

  • Page loading.
  • Website security.
  • Form submissions.
  • Basic navigation.
  • Preventing misuse.

Without these cookies, some parts of the website may not work correctly.

B. Performance and analytics cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use Gulsabi.

They may help us know:

  • Which pages are visited.
  • Which games are played.
  • How long visitors stay.
  • Which devices and browsers are used.
  • Whether pages load correctly.
  • Whether errors happen.

We use this information to improve Gulsabi and fix technical problems.

We do not use this information to create advertising profiles for children.

C. Functionality cookies

These cookies help the website remember basic choices.

They may be used to:

  • Save game progress.
  • Remember display settings.
  • Remember language or simple preferences, if added later.
  • Keep a user’s basic session active, if login features are launched.

D. Embedded content cookies

Gulsabi may include embedded or linked content from third-party platforms, such as YouTube.

When a parent or child plays an embedded video or opens third-party content, that platform may place its own cookies or collect information according to its own privacy policy.

We do not control cookies placed by third-party platforms.

Parents should supervise children when using embedded videos or leaving gulsabi.com.

4. Cookies used for children’s experiences

Gulsabi is designed for children and families.

For children’s experiences, we aim to keep cookies limited and necessary.

We do not knowingly use cookies to:

  • Sell children’s personal information.
  • Show targeted ads to children.
  • Track children across unrelated websites for advertising.
  • Build advertising profiles of children.

5. Game progress and local storage

Some Gulsabi games may save basic progress in the browser.

For example:

  • Score.
  • Level progress.
  • Recently played game state.
  • Basic settings.

This information may stay only on the device and may not be sent to us.

If the browser cache, cookies, or local storage are cleared, game progress may be lost.

6. Newsletter and waitlist cookies

If a parent signs up for a newsletter, waitlist, or update form, cookies may be used to:

  • Confirm form submission.
  • Prevent duplicate submissions.
  • Keep the form working.
  • Help us manage parent communication.

Children should not submit newsletter or waitlist forms without a parent or guardian.

7. Future parent dashboard and AI Buddy cookies

If Gulsabi launches parent accounts, parent dashboards, or AI Buddy features, cookies or local storage may be used to:

  • Keep parents logged in.
  • Save parent-selected settings.
  • Apply child safety controls.
  • Remember topic preferences.
  • Support screen time settings.
  • Maintain security.

If these features are launched, we may update this Cookie Policy with more details.

8. Third-party cookies

Some third-party services may place cookies when used on or through Gulsabi.

These may include:

  • Video platforms.
  • Analytics providers.
  • Email or form tools.
  • Security tools.
  • Hosting or performance tools.

These providers have their own privacy and cookie practices.

We recommend that parents review the privacy settings of browsers, devices, and third-party platforms used by their children.

9. How parents can control cookies

Parents can control cookies through browser settings.

Most browsers allow users to:

  • Block cookies.
  • Delete cookies.
  • Clear local storage.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Receive alerts before cookies are placed.

Browser settings are usually available under “Privacy,” “Security,” or “Site Settings.”

Please note that blocking cookies may affect how Gulsabi works.

Some games, forms, videos, or saved progress features may not work correctly.

10. Do Not Track signals

Some browsers offer “Do Not Track” signals.

Because there is no single accepted standard for these signals, Gulsabi may not respond to them automatically.

Parents can still control cookies using browser settings.

11. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time.

When we update it, we will change the “Last updated” date above.

If we make major changes to how cookies are used for children’s experiences, we will take reasonable steps to update parents where required.
