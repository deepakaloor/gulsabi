From Curio S22 to Earth, Gulsabi came for one mission.

On Curio S22, curiosity is energy. When children stop asking questions, that energy begins to fade.

So he came to Earth. His job is simple: help kids ask Why, What, How and When again.

The mission

Protect the spark of curiosity in every young child.

Gulsabi is made for kids aged 3 to 7, the years when curiosity grows fastest. Kids learn best when they take part, not just watch. A good question gets them thinking, and the answer tends to stick. So every video, game and story is built to leave a child with one more thing to wonder about.

Two modes. One curious hero.

Gulsabi changes with the child’s curiosity.

Buddy Mode

The everyday curiosity friend.

This is the Gulsabi kids meet most. He plays, chats and answers their questions. Along the way he helps with everyday things like washing hands, road safety, good manners, counting, words and first sentences. You’ll find him in the videos, games, stories and books.

Plays & talksHygieneRoad safetyMannersWords & numbers

Super Mode · the thinking superhero

When a child feels stuck, he helps them think.

Say “Gulgul Gulsabi” and Super Mode shows up. He doesn’t fix things by magic. When a child feels scared, confused or stuck, he helps them stop for a second, work out what’s going on, and find a safe way through. Calm, brave and clear.

For parents

Less guilt. More curiosity.

You can’t answer every question the second it’s asked, and that’s okay. The worry was never really the screen time. It’s what’s on the screen. Gulsabi keeps a child’s curiosity going until you can jump in. He’s not here to replace you. He just helps out: a short video, a small game, a little question at a time.
