🛡️ 100% On-Device Privacy Guaranteed

Gulsabi complies strictly with global COPPA and Indian DPDP child safety acts. Every single byte of your child's milestones, nicknames, and playtime counters are kept strictly on this physical phone. We have zero behavioral tracking engines and zero child cloud exposure.

A quick consent

Before we save anything on this device, here’s what Gulsabi stores — and what it never does.

  • Your child’s name/nickname, age band, progress & playtime — kept only on this phone.
  • Your email — used once to verify you’re a grown-up.

We never track your child for ads, or sell or share anything. Cloud sync is optional, off by default, and you can delete it all at any time.
