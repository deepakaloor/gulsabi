Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Gulsabi World, available at gulsabi.com, is operated by Pixnut Media Private Limited.

In this Privacy Policy, “Gulsabi,” “Gulsabi World,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Pixnut Media Private Limited and the Gulsabi World website.

Gulsabi is a children’s edutainment website created for children and families. Your family’s privacy matters to us, and we have written this policy in plain language so it is easy to follow. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how parents can control it, and how to contact us.

We collect as little as we reasonably can, and we use what we do collect to run Gulsabi safely and keep it ad-free for children. Where the law gives you rights over your information, we respect them; where the law allows us to use information to operate, protect, and improve Gulsabi, we may do so.

This policy applies to:

  • gulsabi.com
  • Gulsabi games
  • Gulsabi videos and embedded content
  • Newsletter and waitlist forms
  • Parent dashboard previews
  • Future Gulsabi AI Buddy features, if launched
  • Any other online service that links to this policy

1. Our promise to children and parents

Gulsabi is designed to be a safe, child-friendly space.

We do not:

  • Show third-party advertising inside children’s experiences.
  • Sell personal information.
  • Allow open chat between children.
  • Ask children to create public profiles.
  • Ask children to share their full name, address, phone number, school name, or precise location.
  • Knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
  • Use children’s personal information for targeted advertising.

Most Gulsabi games can be played without creating an account.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to run, protect, and improve Gulsabi.

A. Information parents or guardians provide

We may collect:

  • Email address, when a parent signs up for updates, newsletters, or waitlists.
  • Name, if a parent chooses to provide it.
  • Messages sent through contact forms, email, or social media.
  • Parent dashboard settings, if a parent account feature is launched.
  • Consent records, where parental consent is required.

B. Information from children

Children can use the basic website and games without giving us personal information.

If we launch child profiles, AI Buddy, or parent dashboard features, we may collect limited child-related information only with parent or guardian consent, such as:

  • Child’s first name or nickname.
  • Age range.
  • Parent-selected topics.
  • Parent-selected time limits.
  • Game progress.
  • Quiz scores.
  • Usage time.
  • Learning activity categories, such as stories, rhymes, quizzes, or games.
  • Safety settings selected by the parent.

We will not ask children to submit sensitive personal details.

C. Game and device information

When someone uses Gulsabi, we may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Pages visited.
  • Games played.
  • Browser type.
  • Device type.
  • General country or city-level location based on IP address.
  • Approximate session duration.
  • Error logs.
  • Basic performance data.

This helps us understand whether the website is working properly.

D. Local storage and cookies

Some Gulsabi features may use cookies or local storage.

For example:

  • Saving game progress in the browser.
  • Remembering basic site settings.
  • Supporting website security.
  • Understanding basic site usage.

Some game progress may stay only on the user’s device and may not be sent to us.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not work properly.

E. YouTube and third-party content

Some Gulsabi videos may link to or embed YouTube content. When you play a YouTube video, YouTube may collect information according to its own privacy policy.

We do not control YouTube’s privacy practices.

Parents should supervise children when they leave gulsabi.com or play content on third-party platforms.

F. Future AI Buddy and voice features

Gulsabi may later offer an AI Buddy or voice-based companion.

If this feature launches:

  • A parent or guardian must give consent before a child uses child-specific AI features.
  • Parents will be able to set topics, time limits, and safety controls.
  • Voice or text inputs may be processed to generate responses.
  • We will try to avoid storing full conversation transcripts unless clearly required for safety, support, or a parent-enabled feature.
  • We may store limited activity summaries, such as “story,” “quiz,” or “rhyme,” so parents can understand usage without seeing private child conversations.
  • We will not use a child’s voice or conversation data for advertising.
  • We will not sell child interaction data.

Before launching any AI Buddy feature, we may update this policy with more details.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Operate Gulsabi World.
  • Provide games, videos, and learning content.
  • Send updates to parents who sign up.
  • Respond to messages and support requests.
  • Manage waitlists.
  • Improve safety and age-appropriate content.
  • Detect misuse, security issues, or technical problems.
  • Improve website speed, design, and content.
  • Maintain parent controls, if launched.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Parental consent

Gulsabi is made for young children. We rely on parents and guardians to guide children’s use of the service.

Where required by law, we will seek verifiable parental consent before collecting, using, or sharing personal information from a child.

Parents may:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about their child.
  • Ask us to correct it.
  • Ask us to delete it.
  • Withdraw consent.
  • Stop further collection or use of the child’s personal information.
  • Ask questions about how child data is handled.

To make a request, contact us at:

hello@pixnut.com

We may need to verify that the person making the request is the child’s parent or legal guardian.

5. What we do not use child data for

We do not use child personal information to:

  • Sell products through targeted ads.
  • Build advertising profiles.
  • Sell or rent data.
  • Allow strangers to contact children.
  • Publicly display child profiles.
  • Track children across unrelated websites.
  • Ask for unnecessary personal information.

6. Sharing information

We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us run Gulsabi.

These may include:

  • Website hosting providers.
  • Email newsletter providers.
  • Analytics providers.
  • Security and error-monitoring providers.
  • AI or voice technology providers, if AI Buddy launches.
  • Customer support tools.
  • Legal or professional advisers.

These providers may use the information only to provide services to us. They are not allowed to sell the information or use it for their own advertising.

We may also share information if required by law, court order, government request, or to protect the safety, rights, and security of Gulsabi, children, parents, or others.

7. Analytics

We may use limited analytics to understand how the website is used.

Where possible, we use privacy-friendly settings and avoid using analytics in a way that identifies children personally.

We do not use analytics to show targeted ads to children.

8. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed.

For example:

  • Newsletter email addresses are kept until the parent unsubscribes.
  • Contact messages are kept as long as needed to respond and maintain records.
  • Parent consent records may be kept as required by law.
  • Technical logs are kept for a limited period for security and maintenance.
  • Child-related data, if collected, will be deleted when no longer needed or when a verified parent requests deletion, unless we must keep it for legal or safety reasons.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect information.

These may include:

  • Access controls.
  • Secure hosting.
  • Limited staff access.
  • Encrypted connections where available.
  • Monitoring for misuse.
  • Regular review of data practices.

We work hard to keep information safe, but no website or online service can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. To the extent permitted by law, you share information at your own risk, and parents should help children avoid sharing personal details online.

10. International data transfers

Gulsabi may use service providers located in India or other countries.

When information is processed outside the user’s country, we take reasonable steps to protect it according to applicable law.

11. Your choices

Parents and guardians may:

  • Unsubscribe from emails using the unsubscribe link.
  • Contact us to delete personal information.
  • Disable cookies in browser settings.
  • Stop using the website.
  • Ask us questions about privacy.
  • Withdraw consent for child data processing, where applicable.

12. Children should not contact us alone

If you are a child, please do not send us your name, address, phone number, school name, photo, voice recording, or any private information.

Ask your parent or guardian before contacting us.

13. Links to other websites

Gulsabi may contain links to third-party websites, such as YouTube or social media platforms.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or safety settings of third-party websites.

Parents should review those services before allowing children to use them.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we update it, we will change the “Last updated” date above.

If we make major changes that affect children’s personal information, we will take reasonable steps to notify parents and seek consent where required.
