Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Gulsabi World (“Gulsabi”, “we”, “us”) is a children's edutainment experience created by Pixnut and available at gulsabi.com. We care deeply about the privacy and safety of the children and families who visit us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Our commitment to children

Gulsabi is designed to be a safe, ad-free space for kids. We do not show third-party advertising in children's experiences, we do not sell personal information, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent. Our games are built to be playable without creating an account.

Information we collect

Information you choose to give us

  • Newsletter email: if a grown-up subscribes to updates, we store the email address provided in order to send those updates.
  • Messages: if you contact us, we keep the contents of your message and your contact details so we can reply.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic usage data: we may collect limited, non-identifying analytics (such as pages viewed and device type) to understand how the site is used and to improve it.
  • Cookies: we use a minimal set of cookies and similar technologies that are necessary for the site to function. Game progress (for example, in Memory Match) is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to us.

How we use information

  • To operate, maintain and improve Gulsabi World.
  • To send updates you have asked to receive.
  • To respond to your questions and requests.
  • To keep the experience safe and to prevent misuse.

Third-party services

Some videos are embedded from YouTube using its privacy-enhanced (“no-cookie”) mode. When you play an embedded video, YouTube may receive information in accordance with its own privacy policy. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the practices of third-party services.

Parents' rights and choices

Parents and guardians may ask us to review, correct or delete any personal information we hold that relates to their child, and may withdraw consent at any time. To make a request, contact us at the address below and we will respond promptly.

Data security and retention

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the limited information we hold, and we keep it only for as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes will be highlighted where appropriate.

Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email us at pixnutmedia@gmail.com.
